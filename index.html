Our Story

Born in Damak, Built for Nepal

Mannat Furniture was founded by brothers Manish Panday and Manoj Panday in the heart of Damak, Jhapa — with a single mission: to make beautifully crafted, durable furniture accessible to every Nepali home, hotel, and business.

Growing up surrounded by the lush forests of eastern Nepal, we developed a deep respect for natural materials and skilled craftsmanship. Today, our workshop in Damak brings together traditional Nepali woodworking techniques and contemporary design to create furniture that lasts a lifetime.

From a single dining table to a full hotel fit-out — every order gets the same personal attention. That is the Mannat promise.
